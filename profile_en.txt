Joining Yahoo! Japan in 2006, Masayuki Imamura launched services such as Yahoo! FASHION and X BRAND. In 2009, he became the founder and CTO of VASILY, the technology company behind the online fashion coordination service app, IQON. The company was acquired by Start Today Co., Ltd. in 2017, now known as ZOZO Inc., where Masayuki has been serving as CTO of ZOZO Technologies since 2018. Currently driving a wide range of ZOZO's DX activities, including engineering organization management, information systems, and security risk management for ZOZO.
